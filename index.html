About Grönt

Future Farming

GRÖNT aim to play a pivotal role in solving the global food- livestock feed challenges as our population continues to rise to an estimated 10 Billion people by 2050 and 11 Billion by 2100.

Future Farming

By 2050 we need to produce 60% more food to properly feed the growing population. The scale of the challenge is epic and requires massive amounts of additional land, water and energy which is not available.
GRÖNT challenge the current food- feed systems that leave too much devastation in their wake, rising hunger numbers, 1 in 3 people malnourished, environmental degradation, unsustainable greenhouse gas emissions, inequality, and vulnerability.
We all seek actions that will secure a sustainable future so-called “GAME-CHANGERS”.
In Controlled Environment Agriculture (CEA) and Indoor Vertical Farming (IVF), one of the main “GAME-CHANGERS” would be the development of economically viable systems. That is achieved in our proprietary Nanoponics (colloidal gas) technology growth systems.
CEA contributes substantially to the majority of the 17 UN SDG providing local jobs, economic growth, food security of nutrient-rich fresh & healthy crops, education, sustainable communities, using at least 95% less water, shorten supply chains, eliminates land degradation & is suitable for any climate or region.

AgTech Innovators

GRÖNT develop economically viable Nanoponics technology growth systems providing sustainable, clean, healthy, and nutritious food- feed while using fewer resources than any other CEA or IVF system.

AgTech Innovators

Clients for our systems are any food- feed system developer or operator who aims to improve their systems efficiency & productivity making it a more viable business proposition. The lease or licensing of our systems or technology would allow them to achieve a higher return enabling their business to speed up loss recovery and improve their ROI.
The potential customer segments are broad with system development planned for disaster area, relief camp, farm, greenhouse, home appliance, condominium, supermarket/ mall, fully automated plant factory able to integrate with distribution center, research lab, and deep space exploration.
